What's Next

Future Work
01

CAR-T In Vivo Study

Engineered CAR-T cells currently entering tumor-bearing mice. Testing whether secretory iRGD improves tumor homing and therapeutic efficacy in a live model.

Underway now
02

Anchored iRGD Validation

Larger cohort comparing anchored vs. secretory iRGD directly. Determining whether membrane-tethered iRGD reproduces tumor-penetrating activity after protease cleavage.

Underway now
03

Lentiviral Transduction Panel

Using harvested lentiviral vectors to infect diverse cell types in vitro, then validating expression in vivo — expanding the delivery platform beyond Jurkat and HEK293T.

Underway now
